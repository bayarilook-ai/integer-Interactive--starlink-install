Purpose of this MOP

This Integer Interactive Method of Procedure (MOP) outlines the standardized, step-by-step process for field technicians performing the installation, cabling, and integration of Starlink Gen 3 Terminals at T-Mobile cell sites. It is designed to ensure safe execution, proper hardware mounting per Ericsson guidelines, correct cable terminations, and successful remote validation with T-Mobile MIT Support prior to site closeout.
